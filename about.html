Hi，I'm Xiaobing，a Ph.D student from UMN.

I have a broad range of research interests in sharing economy, revenue management, and network algorithms.

When I was a child, I was curious about new techniques like polar coordinate system transformation. By just considering the same problem from another angle, we can see the essence. Now I am still excited to study new technologies like machine/deep learning, which can help us find the most important information from, maybe, a mess.

Before pursuing my Phd degree, I dreamt of becoming an algorithm developer since I thought it is the most direct way to solve problems. Then I took a class called algorithm analysis which made me realize that with my programming skills, I can only either solve one problem or even one kind of problem, or use the most straightforward way to consider the problem and solve it like doing after-class exercises at school. But I never see the essence of that problem. Maybe I cannot change the world but I hope I can dispel more clouds before us and see the sun much more clearly in this fast-developing time. That’s why I keep learning new things and will never stop.

So now, I love programming but love my research more. Here is my CV.
